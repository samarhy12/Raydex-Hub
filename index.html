RaydexSTS Student Tracking System

Ghana's Basic Schools deserve modern management tools. RaydexSTS is a purpose-built, cloud-based school management platform designed specifically for the Ghanaian education system — from Basic 1 all the way through JHS 3.

Built around GES curriculum frameworks and the BECE structure, it replaces messy spreadsheets, paper registers, and WhatsApp broadcasts with one clean, fast, organised platform. Every school gets its own isolated tenant, its own data, and its own branded portal.

Student Enrolment & Biodata
Complete student profiles with photos, guardian contacts, and full academic history from entry to graduation.
GES-Aligned Grading & Report Cards
Auto-generate end-of-term report cards with class positions, subject remarks, and grade summaries — aligned to GES standards.
CBT Assignments & Assessments
Computer-based tests built in. Teachers create quizzes, students answer on any device — results are instant, no marking required.
Daily Attendance Tracking
Per-class attendance logs with term summaries. Identify patterns, flag chronic absence, and generate printable reports.
In-App Messaging & Announcements
School-wide broadcasts, class notices, and direct messaging between staff — no external apps needed.
Student ID Card Generation
Professionally designed, print-ready student ID cards generated instantly from the system. No Canva, no Photoshop.
Academic Transcripts (PDF Export)
Full transcripts covering each student's entire academic journey at the school — exportable to PDF with one click.
Role-Based Dashboards
Distinct dashboards for headteachers, class teachers, and administrators — each sees only what they need.
